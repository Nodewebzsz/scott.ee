The WordPress of Things

I gave my first ever WordCamp talk on Sunday as part of WordCamp London 2015. I was booked in for a short lightning talk slot on the design track and decided to share some of the experimentation I have been doing with WordPress and the Spark Core.

The Spark Core is an Arduino-compatible, Wi-Fi enabled, cloud-powered development platform that makes creating internet-connected hardware a quick and simple. Most importantly it is open hardware and software — sharing the WordPress philosophy.

My slides are below and I have also added a few of the GitHub links to various experimentations and tools mentioned throughout the talk. If you have any questions ping me on twitter.

  • Sparky — Shortcodes for your Spark Core
